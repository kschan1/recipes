Ingredients:
 - 2 ripe bananas, peeled, coarsely chopped
 - 375ml (1 1/2 cups) skim milk
 - 70g (1/4 cup) low-fat natural yoghurt
 - 2 tablespoons crunchy peanut butter
 - 1 tablespoon honey
 - 1 tablespoon wheat germ
 - 6 ice cubes
 - Honey, extra, to serve

 Directions:
  1. Place the banana, milk, yoghurt, peanut butter, honey, wheat germ and ice in the jug of a blender, and blend until well combined.

  2. Pour among glasses. Drizzle with extra honey to serve.
